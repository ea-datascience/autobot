setStarVRFoveatedFovFactor(f)

 

Set FOV factor for foveated rendering. Defines which percentage of the whole FOV per eye is used for the high resolution area.

Parameters:
  • f (float) - factor in [0.0, 1.0].

Parameters:
  • f (float) - scale factor. 1.0 is 100% of the recommended resolution.
