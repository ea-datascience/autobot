Function Details

observerLookAtPlane(nx, ny, nz, px, py, pz, p1x, p1y, p1z, p2x, p2y, p2z, p3x, p3y, p3z)

 

Sets the lookAt plane for the observer. Two sets of parameters are valid:

observerLookAtPlane(nx,ny,nz,px,py,pz) generates the plane using the plane normal and a point on the plane or

observerLookAtPlane(p1x,p1y,p1z,p2x,p2y,p2z,p3x,p3y,p3z) generates the plane using three points on the plane.

Parameters:
  • nx (float) - The x coordinate of the normal.
  • ny (float) - The y coordinate of the normal.
  • nz (float) - The z coordinate of the normal.
  • px (float) - The x coordinate of the point.
  • py (float) - The y coordinate of the point.
  • pz (float) - The z coordinate of the point.
  • p1x (float) - The x coordinate of the first point.
  • p1y (float) - The y coordinate of the first point.
  • p1z (float) - The z coordinate of the first point.
  • p2x (float) - The x coordinate of the second point.
  • p2y (float) - The y coordinate of the second point.
  • p2z (float) - The z coordinate of the second point.
  • p3x (float) - The x coordinate of the third point.
  • p3y (float) - The y coordinate of the third point.
  • p3z (float) - The z coordinate of the third point.
